BizDirect is a business directory for small and medium (single or co-owned) sized business and freelancers. It is used to locate the nearest businesses to you that offers services you require.

If you own a small or medium sized business or you have partners running your enterprises with you or you are self employed/a freelancer and you want to be easily located by prospective clients near you, this is the place to log in your details and you'd have your phones and email address buzzing with prospective clients.

The world has become smaller as the years have gone by. Your services can go beyond your immediate environment to other localities and even to the rest of the world. Get good reviews and recommendations from your clients and display them produly for prospective clients so you can increase your chances of expanding your clientelle.

The process is simple, easy and straight forward. Sign Up and get your account, profile and resume up and running in minutes. Get reviews and recommendations quickly and see your name come up quickly during searches.

FAQ

Who are the general users of BizDirect?
BizDirect is useful to all owners of small/medium sized, single/co-owned businesses and freelancers. If you are interested in offering your services to people within and outside your location, BizDirect is the platform for you.
Prospective clients will also find this social platform very useful if they are looking for services in any area of need within or outside their community. If you're a client and you're interested in locating a nearby plumber or mechanic, e.t.c., BizDirect is your choice location.
As a business owner or freelancer, what do I need in order to have an account on BizDirect?
All you need to sign up with BizDirect is your business name, description of your business, some contact information and you select a business category your business falls under. You can garnish your profile as you get clients to trust in your business and use your services.
As a prospective client, do I need an account to use BizDirect?
Prospective clients do not need to have accounts with BizDirect to source for possible businesses. You will be able to search for any business of your choice without having to register.
But if you want to recommend or review a business, you will be required to create an account with BizDirect. Also if you're interested in receiving updates on possible businesses of interest to you, it is recommended that you create an account. Click here to get started.
How do I get reviews and recommendations from clients?
Business owners are expected to get their clients to write honest reviews and recommedations for the services they offer to their clients. This should be part of your marketing strategy to your clients.
How does reviews and recommendations affect my business?
Highly reviewed and rated businesses come up first as search results. This means that the more ratings and reviews you get for your business, the higher the chances of your business and services being spotted by prospective clients. And to get high ratings and reviews, your clients must have possitive things to say about their experience working with you.
